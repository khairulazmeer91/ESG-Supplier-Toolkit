Introduction

This Toolkit complements our Supplier Code of Conduct (SCoC) and provides suppliers with practical steps, templates and resources to progressively meet our ESG expectations. It also outlines the data we will require to support NSRF compliance and our public reporting obligations.

Supplier Expectations (Aligned to SCoC)

Sections 3.1 – 3.5

Labour & Human Rights

No forced or child labour, fair wages, working hours compliant with law, non-discrimination, freedom of association. Maintain contracts and payroll records.

Health & Safety

Safe workplace, emergency plans, training, proper worker accommodation where provided (Act 446 compliance where applicable).

Environmental Responsibility

Legal compliance for environmental permits, waste & chemical handling, resource efficiency, and progressive measures for GHG reduction.

Ethics & Governance

Zero tolerance for bribery/corruption, confidentiality & data protection, responsible sourcing and traceability where relevant.

Where gaps are identified, Loob will work collaboratively with suppliers to improve practices and provide targeted support rather than immediate termination for honest non-compliance.

ESG Data We Require (NSRF & Listing Readiness)

We will collect supplier-level ESG data to support NSRF alignment and our public disclosures. The data request will be phased and proportionate to the supplier's size and risk profile.

Environmental

  • Energy consumption (monthly/annual kWh) and fuel usage
  • Water consumption (m³) and any water recycling initiatives
  • Waste generation volumes and disposal routes
  • Valid environmental permits / licenses
  • Certifications (ISO 14001 / MyHIJAU)

Social

  • Workforce size by gender and employment type
  • Wage and overtime policies; sample payslip availability
  • Accident / injury records and remediation actions
  • Number of training hours on safety and ESG topics
  • Grievances and whistleblowing cases

Governance

  • Anti-bribery/anti-corruption policy and training records
  • Data protection/privacy policy
  • Responsible sourcing declarations and traceability
  • ESG or compliance related certifications (ABMS, ISO 37001 etc.)

All data will be treated confidentially and used to produce aggregated metrics for disclosure. Smaller suppliers may submit simplified templates; Loob will provide guidance and support to help suppliers collect the necessary data.

Environmental Readiness & Stewardship Program

Our staged approach helps suppliers progress from awareness to measurable action.

  1. Readiness Survey (Q2 2026) – Quick self-report on energy, water, waste and packaging practices.
  2. Targeted Engagement – Capacity building, workshops and pilot projects for selected suppliers.
  3. Stewardship – Pilot implementation support (e.g., energy audit, packaging trial) and performance monitoring.
  4. Recognition – ‘Sustainability Champion’ status in annual reporting and procurement preference.

Reporting & Transparency

As we prepare for public listing, Loob will disclose aggregated supplier engagement metrics and progress in our annual Sustainability Report. We will publish key indicators such as percentage of suppliers assessed, share of suppliers with environmental management measures, and case studies showcasing improvements.
