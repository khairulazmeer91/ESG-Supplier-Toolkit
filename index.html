Supplier Sustainability Toolkit

This Toolkit complements our Supplier Code of Conduct (SCOC) and provides suppliers with practical steps, templates and resources to progressively meet our ESG expectations. It also outlines the data we will require to support National Sustainability Reporting Framework (NSRF) compliance and our public reporting obligations.

Quick facts

  • ● Aligned with Loob's SCOC and NSRF issued by the Securities Commission Malaysia.
  • ● Toolkit designed to strengthen supplier readiness for future ESG reporting and disclosure.
  • ● Readiness Survey launching in 2026 to assess supplier alignment and data availability.

Supplier Expectations

SCOC Sections 3.1 – 3.5

Labour & Human Rights

No forced or child labour, fair wages, working hours compliant with law, non-discrimination, and freedom of association.

Health & Safety

Safe workplace, emergency plans, workers training, proper worker accommodation where provided (Act 446 compliance where applicable).

Environmental Responsibility

Legal compliance for environmental permits, waste & chemical handling, resource efficiency, and progressive measures for GHG reduction.

Ethics & Governance

Zero tolerance for bribery/corruption, confidentiality & data protection, responsible sourcing and traceability where relevant.

Where gaps are identified, Loob will work collaboratively with suppliers to improve practices and provide targeted support rather than immediate termination for honest non-compliance.

ESG Data We Require

We will collect supplier-level ESG data to support NSRF alignment and our public disclosures. The data request will be phased and proportionate to the supplier's size and risk profile.

Environmental Readiness & Stewardship Program

Our staged approach helps suppliers progress from awareness to measurable action.

Reporting & Transparency

As we prepare for public listing, Loob will disclose aggregated supplier engagement metrics and progress in our annual Sustainability Report. Key indicators include the percentage of suppliers assessed, share of suppliers with environmental management measures, and case studies showcasing improvements.

How We Handle Data

Supplier data is stored securely and used only for aggregated ESG reporting and supplier support. Confidential supplier-level findings are not publicly disclosed without consent.
