ESG Supplier Risk Assessment Guideline

Loob Holding conducts ESG supplier risk assessments to ensure responsible and sustainable business partnerships. The assessment helps us understand potential environmental, social, and governance (ESG) risks across our supply chain, enabling more transparent and resilient supplier relationships.

1. Overview

The ESG Supplier Risk Assessment is a structured evaluation framework that uses both qualitative and quantitative data to determine a supplier’s sustainability risk level. The process includes ESG screening, scoring, and risk classification.

2. Assessment Components

a. ESG Screening Form

Each supplier completes a short screening form consisting of six (6) Yes/No questions under each ESG category: Environmental, Social, and Governance. The form also includes details such as sales agreement and type of product/service supplied.

b. Scoring Matrix

Suppliers are evaluated based on five criteria:

  • Environmental score (E)
  • Social score (S)
  • Governance score (G)
  • Annual sales value to Loob Holding
  • Type of product or service supplied

c. Scoring Scale

Each supplier is assigned a level based on total weighted score:

  • Level 1 – High Risk
  • Level 2 – Medium Risk
  • Level 3 – Low Risk

3. Scoring Formula

Total ESG Risk Score = (E $\times$ w1) + (S $\times$ w2) + (G $\times$ w3) + (Sales $\times$ w4) + (Type $\times$ w5)

where w represents internal weightage values determined by Loob Holding.

4. Risk Classification

Level Risk Category Action / Engagement Approach
Level 1 High Risk Engagement required; may involve corrective action plan or re-evaluation.
Level 2 Medium Risk Continuous monitoring and periodic reassessment.
Level 3 Low Risk Standard due diligence; supplier encouraged to maintain best practices.

5. Review and Update

This ESG supplier risk assessment framework is periodically reviewed to align with evolving sustainability standards and regulatory requirements. Loob Holding reserves the right to update the assessment methodology as necessary.
