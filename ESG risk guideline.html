ESG Risk Assessment Guideline

Loob Holding is committed to responsible and sustainable sourcing practices. Our Supplier ESG Risk Assessment evaluates suppliers’ environmental, social, and governance (ESG) performance to ensure alignment with our sustainability standards.

1. Introduction

Loob Holding is committed to responsible and sustainable sourcing practices. Our Supplier ESG Risk Assessment evaluates suppliers’ environmental, social, and governance (ESG) performance to ensure alignment with our sustainability standards and to identify areas for improvement. The assessment enables Loob to:

Identify potential ESG-related risks in our supply chain.

Support suppliers in improving their ESG maturity.

Strengthen long-term, responsible partnerships.

2. Overview of ESG Risk Assessment Process

Each supplier’s ESG risk is evaluated through five key components:

2.1 Score Components

  • 01 Sales Score: Annual sales value to Loob.
  • 02 Product Score: Criticality level.
  • 03 Environmental: Practices and initiatives.
  • 04 Social Score: Workforce practices.
  • 05 Governance Score: Ethics and compliance.

2.2 Scoring Scale

Score 1 High risk / Weak practice
Score 2 Moderate risk / Partial
Score 3 Low risk / Strong practice

3. Methodology of Scoring

Calculation_v1.0

Overall ESG Risk Score Formula

(Product × w₁) + (Sales × w₂) + (Env × w₃) + (Soc × w₄) + (Gov × w₅)

Weightage (w₁–w₅) is determined based on materiality and internal prioritisation.

5. ESG Risk Categorisation & Recommended Action

HIGH
1.0 – 1.7

Limited ESG awareness.

Action: Prioritised engagement and corrective action plans.

MODERATE
1.8 – 2.4

Partial ESG compliance.

Action: Capacity building and enhanced monitoring.

LOW
2.5 – 3.0

Strong performance.

Action: Recognised as preferred partner / Benchmark supplier.

6. Data Handling

  • • Assessments conducted annually or during onboarding.
  • • Data treated confidentially for internal evaluation.
  • • Periodic updates for certification changes.

7. Continuous Improvement

Loob Holding works collaboratively with suppliers to improve overall sustainability performance. Suppliers with higher risk levels are provided support opportunities to strengthen their ESG capabilities.
